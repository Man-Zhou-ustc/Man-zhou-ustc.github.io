Biography

I am currently a Third-year PhD student University of Science and Technology of China , under the supervision by Asst. Prof. Xueyang Fu. My current research interests span computer vision and machine learning with a series of topics, such as remote sensing image processing, depth super-resolution, and image restoration.


I'm Looking forward to a position for work or postdoc researcher, please feel free to drop me an email if you have the relevant position.
